Who we are

A leading professional platform.

Our vision: to be a leading professional platform that supports and empowers everyone working in — and passionate about — project management and PMOs, raising performance and professional competence across all sectors in support of the Kingdom's Vision and its strategic goals.

The Saudi PMO Community is a professional, knowledge-driven initiative that supports and empowers everyone interested in project management and PMOs across all sectors — an interactive platform bringing together specialists, practitioners, students and enthusiasts, offering a professional environment for exchanging knowledge and experience, developing skills, and advancing modern project-management practice.

Our seal is drawn like the carved motifs of Najdi mud-brick homes — a mark of craft, order, and belonging. It reminds us that the future is always built on foundations.

What we do

Four ways we build the community.

Like a Najdi wall, the community rises brick by brick — each layer supporting the next.

01

Connect

We build a professional network that brings specialists, practitioners and enthusiasts together to exchange experience and lessons learned.

02

Learn

We spread specialised knowledge and raise awareness of how project management improves organisational performance — through webinars, lectures and expert content.

03

Grow

We develop members' theoretical and practical skills through knowledge, training and professional programmes suited to every level.

04

Empower

We support and empower national talent and everyone working in the field — raising professional maturity in support of the goals of the Kingdom's Vision.
